Our Story

We are an independent coffee roaster and coffeehouse in downtown Honolulu. Since 2011, we've specialized in sourcing and roasting coffee grown right here in Hawaii — bringing out the sweetness, origin character, and varietal nuance of each bean.

We serve 100% Hawaii coffee and espresso, Japanese matcha, matcha tortes, and fresh pastries. Every cup is a taste of the islands.

— Fred, Fumiko, Jon, Alyssa, Kaya & Karol
